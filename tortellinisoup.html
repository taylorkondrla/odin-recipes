Taylor's Tortellini Soup

Decription

This is Taylor's favorite soup that she makes all the time in the fall

Ingredients

  • Tortellini
  • Sausage
  • Kale
  • Chicken Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Potatos

Instructions

  1. Brown Sausage
  2. Cook onions, garlic, celery, carrots, potatoes
  3. Add broth, heavy cream, and kale
  4. Simmer for 35 Minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!
