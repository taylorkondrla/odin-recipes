Taylor's Famous Blueberry Pound Cake

Decription

This is one of the best desserts, it is a fan favorite for anyone who tries it!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Blueberries

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs, butter, and sugar
  2. Mix in flour until combined
  3. Add blueberries
  4. Bake at 400 for 45 minutes
  5. Enjoy! Best served with vanilla ice cream!
