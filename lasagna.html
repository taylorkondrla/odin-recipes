Taylor's World Famous Lasagna

Decription

Lasagna is a great filling Italian dinner loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Riccata Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Brown ground beef
  3. Layer noodles, riccata, and sauce in dish
  4. Add mozzerella on top
  5. Bake at 375 for 40 minutes
